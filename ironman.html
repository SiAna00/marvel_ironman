Iron man

About Iron man

Iron man's real name was Anthony Edwards "Tony" Stark. He was a billionaire businessman, a former CEO of Stark Industries and one of the most powerful people in the world. While captured by a terrorist organization Tony Stark created an armoured suit to help him survive and escape. Upon returning home, Stark continued to use the suit to fight the bad guys and protect Earth from its enemies. He soon revealed himself to the public as Ironman.
